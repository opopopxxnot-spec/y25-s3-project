      Compare parties side-by-side

      Neutral checklist to help you compare policy, governance record, finances & promises.

      Checklist

      • Has the party published a detailed manifesto / costed plan?
      • Candidate/funding transparency (affidavits on MyNeta / ADR)
      • Legislative record (PRS reports on bills & attendance)
      • Independent fact-checks for big claims (AltNews / Factly)
      • Recent reliable news analysis (The Indian Express / The Hindu)

      How to judge sources

      • Prefer official documents (ECI affidavits) for candidate facts
      • Cross-check claims across 2+ reputable outlets
      • Use fact-checkers to debunk viral misinformation
      • Beware of echo chambers and paid/social amplification
